About FrGDPS Demonlist

Community platform to categorize and rank the hardest levels in FrGDPS

What is Demonlist?

Demonlist is a list of the hardest demon levels in Geometry Dash sorted by difficulty. Unlike the official in-game level list, the demonlist is created and maintained by the player community.

FrGDPS Demonlist focuses specifically on demon levels created for FrGDPS private servers, with an independent ranking and verification system.

Purpose of This Website

FrGDPS Demonlist was created with several main goals in mind:

  • Provide trusted information about the hardest demon levels in FrGDPS
  • Recognize the achievements of players who have completed difficult levels
  • Encourage healthy competition among players to achieve the highest ranking
  • Provide a platform to share proof of level completion (verification)
  • Help new players find challenges that suit their skills

Difficulty System

We use a demon classification system with 5 main difficulty levels:

Easy Demon

Beginner level demons, suitable for those who are just starting to play demons. Example: The Nightmare, The Lightning Road

Medium Demon

Medium difficulty demons, require a solid foundation. Example: B, Platinum Adventure

Hard Demon

Challenging demons with complex gameplay. Example: Nine Circles, Jawbreaker

Insane Demon

Demons are very difficult, only for experienced players. Example: Windy Landscape, Supersonic

Extreme Demon

The highest level, only the hardest levels fall into this category. Example: Bloodbath, Zodiac

Pemon List

The Pemon List is a separate ranking system for platformer levels in FrGDPS. These levels focus on precise platforming mechanics rather than traditional demon gameplay.

Platformer levels are evaluated based on:

  • Platforming difficulty and precision required
  • Level design and creativity
  • Length and complexity
  • Visual aesthetics and decoration

Impossible List

The Impossible List contains the most challenging levels in FrGDPS that push the boundaries of what's possible in the game.

These levels are characterized by:

  • Extreme difficulty that requires near-perfect execution
  • Complex gameplay mechanics and patterns
  • High skill ceiling with minimal room for error
  • Often featuring unique gameplay elements not seen in other levels

FAQ

Q: How to submit level to demonlist?
A: Use the submit page on this website by completing all the required information.

Q: What are the level requirements to enter the demonlist?
A: Level must be demon, have video verification, and pass the assessment of our verification team.

Q: How does the ranking system work?
A: Rankings are based on a combination of technical difficulty, level length, and community consensus.

Q: Can I contribute to the list?
A: Yes! We welcome community contributions. You can submit levels, verify completions, or provide feedback on existing rankings.

Q: How often is the list updated?
A: The list is updated regularly as new levels are created and verified. Check the recent updates section for the latest changes.
